Level1 - Office and school ​

询问他人意见 ​

通常可以使用 How 来询问他人意见。

比如：How's your teacher?

How are your teachers?

Positive response:

They're good.

She's great.

Neutral response:

He's OK.

They're so-so.

Negative response:

He's bad.

They're terrible.

介绍人或物 ​

可以使用 This is 或者 That is

This is my desk. / 否定：This is not my desk.

That is John. / 否定：That is not John.

this 用来指代离的比较近的人或物，that 用来指代离的比较远的人或物。

将 is 放在句首可以变成一般疑问句用于询问。

Is this your desk?

Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

询问某人的年龄 ​

How old are you?

How old is she / he?

回答的时候可以只给出数字，也可以在后面加上 years old

英文文化： 在英文国家，询问某人的年龄被认为是粗鲁的。

课堂笔记： ​

自我介绍环节中：

提到希望可以与国外的朋友流利的用英文交流，可以用英文思维进行回答。

I want to speak English well.

I want to speak with foreigners.

词汇扩展：

mailbox 邮箱

poster 海报

banner 横幅

calculate 计算 / calendar 日历

front desk 前台

口语交流（Part 1）：

Lily is Paul's daughter. / Lily is Paul's little girl. （国外形容女儿更倾向于使用 little girl）

儿子可以使用 little boy 代替 son，口语当中。

Paul is Lily's father/dad/papa/daddy.

Joe looks like Paul's father.

looks like：长得像，可能是某个人长得像另一个人。

如果想准确表述看起来像，可以使用：Joe seems like Paul's father.

I think Musa is Joe's father.

不确定的时候，口语也可以说：I guess Musa is Joe's father. （更多表达不确定，而不是我猜）

My guess is that Musa is Joe's father.

Joe is 55 years old.

年龄是猜测的，并不是实际知道了解的时候，可以表述为：Joe is

about/around 50/55 years old.

Joe is in his fifties. 他处于50多岁。

Joe is in his early fifties. / Joe is in his mid-fifties. / Joe is in his late fifties.

I guess Paul is 32 years old.

I guess Paul is in his early thirties.

Lily seems like a 7-year-old.

Lily seems 7 years old.

Lily seems like she is 7 (years old).

I think Musa is younger than Joe, I guess he is 46 years old.

She is one/a year older than me.

I guess he was born in 1970s. 我猜想他出生于70年代。

I believe he was born in 1975. believe 是确信，语义更为强烈。

consider means think about carefully

口语交流（Part 2）：

Q: Thanks for showing me the office.

A: You're welcome. / My pleasure. / It's my pleasure. / It's my honor. / I'm happy to help.

Q: It such a big office.

A: Yeah, that's a very big one. / Yeah, let me introduce the office. / Yeah, we have many desks and computers here. / Yeah, it's quite a large.

There is an elevator over here.

仅仅只有一台：Yes, just one. / Yes, only one.

There are some restrooms in different places.

It's near the manager's office.

It's close to the manager's office.

可以使用问题中的某些词语。

It's behind of the manager's office. / The manager's office is in front of the restroom.

如果未听清需要重复，可以使用

Sorry please say it again.

Excuse me?

Pardon?

There are many new coffee cups prepared for you in the kitchen.

Prepare for 用于老板什么的，会表现的比较生疏

You can use the new coffee cups over there.

and so on. and things like that 等等，诸如此类。
